Net of the Rectangular Prism

The net shows how the rectangular prism unfolds into a flat pattern. The six rectangular faces (top, bottom, front, back, left, and right) are connected along the fold lines (shown in yellow dashes). Opposite faces share the same color: front and back are blue (largest faces), top and bottom are green (smallest faces), and left and right are red (medium faces).
